Meet The Writers

Mike Blake

Producer · Composer · Arranger
Programmer · Vocalist

Mike brings the sound, the structure and the production. With live experience and a producer's ear, he shapes each song from the ground up.

Mike Shannon

Lyricist · Songwriter
Creative Collaborator

Selected Credits & Collaborations

On The Beach

Co-wrote the Sonic Palms single. Top 40 Austria, #1 Jordan, and Top 10 in various European dance charts.

Jenny Frost

Co-wrote, co-produced and recorded a solo song for Jenny Frost of Atomic Kitten.

Anything But Monday

Wrote and recorded 20 songs for the American girl group ABM.

Everybody Samba

Co-wrote Loona & DJ Big Tim's Euro 2024 song.

Loona

Wrote two songs for Loona's album Moonrise.

Mark Hill

Worked alongside Mark Hill at Stop, Drop and Roll, on cuts for The Sugababes.

Summer Dreaming

Wrote and produced a single for Westwood, the voice of Basshunter's “Now You're Gone”.

Maycke

Wrote and produced an album for the winner of the Dutch television talent show Holland’s Biggest Popstar.

J-Dash

Co-wrote an unreleased song with the American rapper, songwriter, record producer and film composer.
