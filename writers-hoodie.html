Studio Hoodie

A relaxed black pullover hoodie developed around an understated Marveltonez chest mark and an easy, studio-ready fit.

Coming Soon

Product Preview

Designed as a versatile layer for writing rooms, rehearsals, travel and everyday wear, the Studio Hoodie keeps the Marveltonez identity deliberately understated with a compact chest mark.

The classic pullover silhouette includes a practical front pouch pocket, ribbed cuffs and hem, and a soft hood with drawcords. The relaxed unisex presentation is intended to suit different styles and settings.

Final fabric composition, garment weight, size guide, care details, price and launch availability will be confirmed after the production sample is approved.
